About Me

Gustavo Nieves is a serial entrepreneur and an accounting and operations specialist. Gustavo has an accounting degree from Liberty University. Since 2006 Gustavo has owned companies in the IT, web, advertising, accounting, tax, and food service industries.

Gustavo is currently a principal at Dynamic Ventures Group, Inc., a company that owns several like-minded organizations in the advertising and technology industries. In his role as principal for DVG, Gustavo manages the operations, finance and accounting of the two advertising agency teams as well as the Audio/Visual/Lighting business unit. In addition to his leadership roles in operations and finance, Gustavo provides strategic insights into the Hispanic consumer at DVG's Hispanic market agency, UniComm Media Group.

Gustavo also owns a CPA firm that provides accounting, consulting and fractional CFO services. He also has an interest in a small pastry shop in Upstate South Carolina.

Gustavo currently serves on the board of directors of CommunityWorks Carolina and CommunityWorks Federal Credit Union. He also serves as the chair of the audit committee of CommunityWorks Federal Credit Union. Gustavo also volunteers with the SC Hispanic Chamber of Commerce as an entrepreneurship instructor.

Gustavo speaks to entrepreneurs on topics including project management, agile/Scrum project management, operations management, accounting, entrepreneurship, financial management, and corporate finance.
